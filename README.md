## üßä Gameplay Mechanics

The character can move in **four directions**: up, down, left, and right.  
Once a direction is chosen:

1. The character automatically slides along the ice.  
2. They cannot stop or change direction until they **collide with a wall, rock, or object**.  
3. The player must plan the correct path to reach the goal (point B).

Completing a level unlocks the next one.
